I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Connecticut. I am currently working with Professor Mohammad Maifi Hasan Khan. My research interests are in the area of cloud systems and scalable real-time integration of sensor streams in the cloud, with a focus on resource allocation and performance modeling. I am also interested in investigating tools and algorithms which help users to understand the relations between configuration settings and the performance of a system. I completed my B.E. in Computer Science from School of Information and Communication Technology, Hanoi University of Science and Technology, Vietnam in 2010.
